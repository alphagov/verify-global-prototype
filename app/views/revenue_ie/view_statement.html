View your End of year statement (P21)

How to view your End of year statement

My Documents is a facility in myAccount to store certain documents and is accessible on the ribbon running across the top of the myAccount home page. If you are a Pay As You Earn (PAYE) customer you will be able to view, download or print End of year statements (P21) from My Documents.

If you have any unread documentation, an orange circle will display on the My Documents folder icon.

Any End of year statements (P21) on your record going back more than the last 4 years will be stored in the Archive folder.

How to access the service

You must be registered for myAccount to access My Documents:

  • click on ‘My Documents’ on the myAccount home page
  • select the specific year folder from the ‘Summary’ screen
  • view, download or print from My Documents.

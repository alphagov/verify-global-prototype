Finding the right company to verify you

Certified companies have built new, secure systems to verify identities. These systems work by checking:

  • your identity documents are valid
  • it’s you providing your details, and not someone pretending to be you

However, each company uses different methods for each of these steps.

So we’ll now ask you some questions to check which companies can verify you. We’ll then send you to the website of the company you choose.
